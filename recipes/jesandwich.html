Japanese Egg Sandwich

Description

The Japanese Egg Salad Sandwich, or Tamago Sando, is a delightful breakfast classic. With creamy egg salad made flavorful by Kewpie mayo, it's sandwiched between soft milk bread slices. This simple yet delicious combination offers a taste of Japan's culinary charm, perfect for a satisfying and easy morning treat.

Ingredients

  • 4 hard boiled eggs
  • 6 white bread pan
  • 3 tbsp of Kewpie mayonnaise
  • a pinch of salt
  • ½ tsp of sugar
  • 2 tsp of milk
  • salted butter

Steps

  1. Transfer your peeled eggs in a bowl and mash it with a fork. Note: make sure to mash the egg whites into small, same-size pieces.
  2. Add the sugar, salt, mayonnaise and milk all at once then mix until well combined.
  3. Now that you have your egg mixture, let’s assemble.
  4. Grab your bread and spread the butter on one side. Do this on all of the bread.
  5. Spread your egg mixture evenly on the buttered side of the bread.
  6. Put the other side of bread on top and push gently until it flattens.
  7. This next step is optional. With a knife, cut all sides of crusts off the bread.
  8. And lastly, cut the bread in half then serve.
